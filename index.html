History and Milestones

  • June 2003

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Owner Sal Casillas starts out his septic service business by taking an 4-day family caravan trip to Texarkana, Arkansas from Salinas, California to purchase their first red 1987 International DT 466 Vacuum Truck

  • February 2016

    A New Conversation is Born

    Sal Casillas Jr. attends the Water and Wastewater Equipment, Treatment & Transport Show (WWETT) in Indianapolis, Indiana and pitches the first on-demand septic tank cleaning application to attendees.

  • May 2016

    Proof of Concept

    PumperPal beats out 29 entrants and becomes a finalist for the Startup Challenge Monterey Bay competition hosted by CSU Monterey Bay's Institute for Innovative and Economic Development.

  • June 2016

    Phase Two Expansion

    PumperPal introduces it's platform to pumping companies across the US in the Pumper Magazine.
